Vendor Waste Disposal Agreement Summary

The organization engages external vendors for periodic waste collection and disposal.
Vendors are selected based on availability and service cost.

Plastic waste and electronic waste are handed over to vendors during scheduled pickups.
Invoices and service receipts are maintained for accounting purposes.

Formal verification of vendor authorization or recycling certifications is not
consistently documented. Vendor performance is reviewed informally based on service
regularity and operational convenience.
